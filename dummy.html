Our Student's Success Stories

Cindy De Vega

Cindy, a ScholarLink student, had a dream of attending a prestigious university. With the help of her mentor, she was able to create a strong academic plan, develop her leadership skills, and secure a full scholarship to her dream school. Cindy is now studying to become a doctor and is grateful for the support she received from ScholarLink.

Jann, a ScholarLink mentee, was once a struggling high school student from an underprivileged community. He recently graduated with honors and is now working as a research assistant, paving the way for a better future for his family.
